## About This MicroSim

This visualization shows the progression of educational grade levels from early childhood through adult learning, highlighting the characteristics that define each stage.

### Learning Objectives

1. Classify educational content by appropriate grade level
2. Identify characteristics that define each educational stage
3. Match content complexity to audience developmental level

## Lesson Plan

**Grade Level:** Undergraduate (Education majors)
**Duration:** 15 minutes

### Procedure

1. Explore each grade level by hovering over the nodes
2. Click nodes to see example MicroSim topics
3. Note the progression from concrete to abstract thinking
