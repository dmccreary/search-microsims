## About This MicroSim

This interactive pyramid displays the six levels of Bloom's Revised Taxonomy (2001), from Remember at the foundation to Create at the apex.

### Learning Objectives

1. Identify the six levels of Bloom's Taxonomy in order
2. Recognize key verbs associated with each cognitive level
3. Match MicroSim activity types to appropriate Bloom levels

## Lesson Plan

**Grade Level:** High School / Undergraduate
**Duration:** 15 minutes

### Procedure

1. Hover over each pyramid level to see verbs and examples
2. Toggle "Show MicroSim Types" for activity suggestions
3. Note the LOTS/HOTS distinction on the right
